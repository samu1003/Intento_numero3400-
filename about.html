About Us

Founded in Caracas, Venezuela in 2020, Zago Pastry and Bakery was created by Chef Fernando with a passion for artisan baking and authentic Venezuelan flavors.

Our mission is to bring the warmth and tradition of Venezuelan pastries directly to our customers. Every product is carefully crafted using high-quality ingredients and time-honored techniques, ensuring a unique and authentic taste experience.

As an online bakery, we make it easy for customers to explore our menu, place orders, and enjoy freshly baked desserts without leaving home. From classic sweets to modern creations, every item reflects dedication, quality, and creativity.

At Zago Bakery, we are more than just a bakery — we are a space where culture, flavor, and passion come together to create unforgettable moments.
